# Additional AI Features to Add (Workflow + Inventory Scope)

> Scope note: these ideas are intentionally limited to internal intake, handling, catalog prep, and inventory workflow.

## 1) AI Lot Title Optimizer
Generate 3-5 title variants per item with character limits tuned for auction marketplaces.

## 2) AI SEO Tag Expander
Convert basic tags into long-tail searchable phrases and buyer-intent keywords.

## 3) AI Bundle/Split Recommender
Analyze similar items and suggest whether they should be sold as a lot, pair, or single listing.

## 4) AI Missing-Field Assistant
Detect incomplete item records and suggest probable values (category, era, dimensions, condition).

## 5) AI Stage Triage Prioritizer
Rank items by urgency (high value, event deadline, stalled stage, repair risk) for daily work queues.

## 6) AI Auto-QA for Descriptions
Check generated descriptions for prohibited claims, overstatements, missing condition disclaimers, and grammar.

## 7) AI Fraud/Risk Signal
Flag suspicious consignments (inconsistent provenance text, unusual value claims, duplicate photos).

## 8) AI Price Confidence Scoring
Attach confidence score and rationale to estimated values using historical studio sell-through data.

## 9) AI Stage Delay Forecast
Predict which items are likely to get stuck before photography/prep and suggest interventions.

## 10) AI Photo Shot-List Planner
Given category + item style, produce a required shot plan (hero, detail, damage, scale reference).

## 11) AI Damage Progress Tracker
Compare successive item photos over time to detect new damage during handling.

## 12) AI Consigner Intake Chat
Guided conversational intake assistant that pre-qualifies items before studio drop-off.

## 13) AI Event Catalog Theme Builder
Group eligible items into themed events (mid-century, rustic, luxury decor) with rationale.

## 14) AI Catalog Consistency Enforcer
Standardize naming conventions, capitalization, material references, and measurement format across inventory.

## 15) AI Daily Workflow Brief
Generate a start-of-day operational brief: priority items, repairs due, missing photos, and bottlenecks by stage.
